The Olive Green

" The Olive Green retains its heritage and charm , yet offering a fabulous Italian culinary experience, surprising you on every visit"

The Olive Green is a fictional italian restaurant located in Nuremberg, Germany. It is an elegant old villa transformed into a stunning venue with an incredible lounge bar and a spacious yet intimate dining space. It retains its heritage and charm and offers a delicious selection of cocktails, wines and a menu curated by our talented chefs. Head to Olive Green for a leisure lunch, a romantic dinner for two under the stars, or an evening out with friends at our alfresco bar.
